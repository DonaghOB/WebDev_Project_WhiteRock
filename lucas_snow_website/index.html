Zermatt, Switzerland

Nestled in the Swiss Alps, Zermatt is a picturesque village known for its stunning scenery and the iconic Matterhorn mountain. Skiers and snowboarders can enjoy a variety of slopes, from gentle beginner runs to challenging off-piste terrain. The car-free village adds to the charm, making it a perfect destination for a cozy winter getaway.
